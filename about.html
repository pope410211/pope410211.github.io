Hi there! Welcome to my website! My name is Nick, dungeon master, game master, and future internet lord (aka Front End Engineer). I recently graduated from The Iron Yard -- Orlando and looking forward to making the internet a better place ... well just making the internet.

I come from the world of hospitality, and spent 14 years in the industry, working every position imaginable in full service restaurants and resorts to McDonalds, with the last 6 years in management and earning multiple awards. But after all of that, it was brought to my attention that I spent all of my free time toying with tech - building computers, learning to code, repairing old NES systems all for fun. That is when I decided with my Fiancée Candace to really learn to code, and take my past time passions into a career and attended The Iron Yard -- Orlando. I still have a lot experience to earn, however in the short amount of time I have been in this industry it has become clear that I made the right decision and look forward to a fulfilling career.

I one day hope to work for Nintendo of America and/or develop a game for one of their consoles. However in the meantime have a look at my resume, or look for me studying JavaScript.
